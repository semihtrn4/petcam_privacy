Privacy Policy — Pet Camera App

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This privacy notice for PetCam ("we," "us," or "our") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. PetCam is the ultimate pet camera app designed to capture stunning pet photos and portraits — featuring an attention-grabbing pet sounds library, custom sound recording, and built-in video recording.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

Summary of Key Points

  • We collect device identifiers and purchase data primarily through RevenueCat to manage your subscription.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising.
  • Custom sounds you record are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Photos and videos you capture are saved to your device only — we do not access or collect them.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18 years of age.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Data Collected Automatically

  • Device ID — Used by RevenueCat to identify your device for subscription management.
  • User ID — A RevenueCat-assigned identifier to link your account with your subscription status.
  • Purchase & Subscription History — Records of your PetCam Pro subscription processed via Apple App Store and RevenueCat.
  • Product Interaction Data — Anonymized data about how you use the app (e.g., sounds played, camera features used) to improve the platform.
  • Crash & Performance Data — Technical diagnostics including crash logs, launch times, and performance metrics to ensure app stability.

Data You Provide

  • PetCam does not require account registration or login.
  • If you contact our support team, we may collect your email address and the content of your message solely to respond to your inquiry.

Photos, Videos & Custom Sounds

📸 All photos and videos captured with PetCam are saved directly to your device (Camera Roll / Photos library). We do not upload, access, or store your photos or videos on our servers.

🎙️ Custom sounds you record within PetCam are stored locally on your device only. They are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Microphone & Camera Access

PetCam requests access to your device's microphone (to record custom sounds) and camera (to capture pet photos and videos). These permissions are used exclusively for the core functionality of the app. You may revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings.

Payment Information

All payments are processed directly through the Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment card details, bank information, or billing address. RevenueCat receives only confirmation of your subscription status.

3. How Do We Use Your Information?

  • To verify and manage your PetCam Pro subscription status
  • To unlock premium features for Pro subscribers
  • To improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns and plan new features
  • To respond to customer support inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management. Collects Device ID, User ID, and purchase history. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →
  • Apple App Store — Payment processing and subscription verification.

We do not integrate advertising networks, analytics SDKs that track users across apps, or data brokers.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Turkey, we process your data based on:

  • Performance of a contract — To deliver your PetCam Pro subscription
  • Legitimate interests — To improve app stability and performance
  • Legal obligations — To comply with applicable laws
  • Your consent — Where explicitly required (e.g., microphone and camera access)

6. When Do We Share Your Information?

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — RevenueCat, as described above, to manage subscriptions
  • Legal requirements — When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers — In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Fraud prevention — To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Subscription records managed by RevenueCat are retained in accordance with their data retention policy. Anonymized usage and diagnostic data may be retained for up to 12 months.

8. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

9. Do We Collect Data from Minors?

PetCam is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (including revoking camera/microphone permissions via device Settings)
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

11. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please contact us at the email address below. We will process your request within 30 days. Note that some data may need to be retained for legal compliance purposes.

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The PetCam mobile app does not use browser cookies. Standard mobile identifiers (Device ID) are used solely for subscription management as described above. We do not use cross-app tracking technologies or advertising identifiers (IDFA) for ad targeting.

13. Do-Not-Track

As PetCam does not engage in cross-site tracking, Do-Not-Track signals are not applicable to our services.

14. US Privacy Rights

If you are a US resident, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. We do not sell personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) or similar state laws.

15. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
